Detailed Information about Plants 🌱

  1. Photosynthesis: The fundamental process where leaves, acting as food factories, convert light energy into chemical energy.
  2. Essential Nutrients: Plants require sunlight, water, air, and nutrients : Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium to grow.
  3. Root & Stem Function: Roots anchor plants and absorb water/minerals, while stems transport these nutrients and provide structural support.
  4. Transpiration: Plants release water vapor through their leaves, which cools the plant and contributes to the water cy cle.
  5. Respiration & Oxygen: While plants produce oxygen during the day via photosynthesis, they also consume oxygen
  6. Vascular System: Most plants have vascular tissues (xylem and phloem) that transport water, salts, and sugars throughout the plant.
  7. Flowering Plants (Angiosperms): These constitute 90% of plant life and are essential for food production and ecosystem stability.
  8. Soil Conservation: Plant roots hold soil in place, preventing erosion and maintaining fertility. Environmental Indicators: Plants act as bioindicators, helping to assess environmental health and changes.
