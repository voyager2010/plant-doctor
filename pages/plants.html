Money Plant

Money Plant is a popular indoor
plant known for its beauty and
easy care. It is believed to bring
positivity and good luck.

Snake Plant

Snake Plant is a very easy-to-grow indoor plant.
It is perfect for beginners because it needs very little care
and can survive in low light conditions.

Ananta Plant

Ananta, also known as Gardenia jasminoides
or Gandhraj, is a popular evergreen shrub
celebrated for its highly fragrant,
creamy-white flowers and glossy, dark green leaves.

Jade Plant

The Jade Plant is a popular succulent
known for its thick, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves.
The leaves are usually deep green and
may develop red edges when exposed to sunlight.

Adenium Plant

Adenium, commonly known as Desert Rose,
is a beautiful flowering succulent plant.
It is famous for its thick, swollen trunk
(called caudex) and bright, colorful flowers.
