Ananta Plant

Ananta, also known as Gardenia jasminoides
or Gandhraj, is a popular evergreen shrub
celebrated for its highly fragrant,
creamy-white flowers and glossy, dark green leaves.

Tulsi Plant

Tulsi, also known as Holy Basil, is a sacred
and medicinal plant in India.
It is widely used in Ayurveda and helps improve health and immunity.

Adulsa Plant

Adulsa, also known as Adhatoda,
is a medicinal plant commonly used in Ayurveda.
It is very useful for treating cough,
cold, and respiratory problems.
