Jade Plant

🌿 Jade Plant (Basic Info)

Common Name: Jade Plant
Scientific Name: Crassula ovata
Other Names: Money Tree, Lucky Plant
Family: Crassulaceae
Origin: South Africa

🌿 Detailed Information

Scientific Name: Crassula ovata

The Jade Plant is a succulent plant with thick, fleshy leaves and strong stems. The leaves are green and shiny, sometimes with red edges.

It stores water in its leaves, which helps it survive in dry conditions. This makes it easy to care for and perfect for beginners.

The plant grows slowly and can develop into a small tree indoors. It prefers bright sunlight and well-draining soil.

The Jade Plant is also believed to bring good luck and prosperity, making it popular in homes and offices.

Overall, it is a low-maintenance and long-lasting plant that adds beauty and greenery to indoor spaces.

🌱 Care Guide

  • Water: Every 1–2 weeks
  • Light: Bright sunlight
  • Temperature: 18°C – 30°C
  • Soil: Well-draining soil

🌿 Fertilizer

Feed once a month during growing season.

✂️ Maintenance

  • Trim to maintain shape
  • Remove dead leaves

🌱 Propagation

  1. Cut a leaf or stem
  2. Let it dry
  3. Plant in soil

⚠️ Common Problems

  • Yellow Leaves: Overwatering
  • Wrinkled Leaves: Underwatering
  • Pests: Use neem oil

🌼 Benefits

  • Easy to grow
  • Long-lasting plant
  • Decorative and attractive
