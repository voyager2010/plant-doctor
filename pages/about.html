🌱 About This Website

Plant Doctor is a simple website created to help people take care of their plants. It provides easy information about different plants, their care, and solutions to common problems.

🎯 Our Purpose

The main goal of this website is to make plant care easy for beginners. Many people face problems like yellow leaves, slow growth, or drying plants. Plant Doctor helps you understand these problems and gives simple solutions.

🌟 What You Can Find Here

  • Information about different plants
  • Watering and sunlight guidance
  • Plant problems and solutions
  • Useful plant care tips
