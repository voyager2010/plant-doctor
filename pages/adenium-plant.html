Adenium Plant

🌿 Adenium Plant (Basic Info)

Common Name: Adenium, Desert Rose
Scientific Name: Adenium obesum
Family: Apocynaceae
Origin: Africa and the Middle East

🌿 Detailed Information

Scientific Name: Adenium obesum

The Adenium plant, also known as Desert Rose, is a succulent with a thick stem and beautiful colorful flowers. It is widely used as a decorative plant.

It stores water in its stem, which helps it survive in dry conditions. This makes it drought-tolerant and easy to maintain.

The plant has a bonsai-like appearance and grows best in bright sunlight with well-draining soil.

It produces attractive flowers in shades of pink, red, and white during the growing season.

With proper care, the Adenium plant can live for many years and add beauty to indoor and outdoor spaces.

🌱 Care Guide

  • Water: Once a week
  • Light: Full sunlight
  • Temperature: 20°C – 35°C
  • Soil: Well-draining soil

🌿 Fertilizer

Feed every 2–3 weeks during growing season.

✂️ Maintenance

  • Prune to shape
  • Remove dead branches

🌱 Propagation

  1. Use seeds or cuttings
  2. Plant in dry soil
  3. Water lightly

⚠️ Common Problems

  • Yellow Leaves: Overwatering
  • No Flowers: Lack of sunlight
  • Pests: Use neem oil

🌼 Benefits

  • Beautiful flowering plant
  • Decorative bonsai look
  • Easy to maintain
