Aloe Vera Plant Tips

    🌿 🌟 Aloe Vera Tips

  • Water only once a week (do not overwater)
  • Keep in bright sunlight but avoid very strong direct sun
  • Use well-drained sandy soil
  • Do not keep water in pot (avoid root rot)
  • Remove damaged leaves regularly
    • 👉 Important: Overwatering is the biggest problem for Aloe Vera

Snake Plant Tips

🌿 🌟 Snake Plant Tips

  • Water every 10–12 days (very less water needed)
  • Can grow in low light or bright light
  • Use dry and well-drained soil
  • Avoid frequent watering
  • Perfect indoor plant
    • 👉 Important: This plant survives even if you forget to water 😄

Money Plant Tips

🌿 🌟 Money Plant Tips

  • Water 2–3 times a week
  • Keep in indirect sunlight (not direct sun)
  • Can grow in water or soil
  • Cut long stems to promote growth
  • Keep soil slightly moist
    • 👉 Important: Too much sunlight can burn leaves

Tulsi Plant Tips

🌿 🌟 Tulsi Plant Tips

  • Water daily but in small amount
  • Needs full sunlight
  • Keep in open area (balcony/garden)
  • Trim leaves regularly
  • Protect from strong wind
    • 👉 Important: Tulsi needs fresh air and sunlight

Adulsa Plant Tips

🌿 🌟 Adulsa Plant Tips

  • Water 2–3 times a week
  • Needs full or partial sunlight
  • Use normal garden soil
  • Trim plant to keep healthy
  • Use organic fertilizer
    • 👉 Important: Good sunlight helps in better medicinal growth
